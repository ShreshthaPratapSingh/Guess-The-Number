Hey guys, so this game is all about your Luck. You can test out your luck here by guessing a number. If the number matches the number chosen by the system, you will be awarded points accordingly. After every attempt you will recieve a hint but with each hint your score which will be intially set to 100 will be reduced by 10 points each attempt. So you will have 10 attempts to guess. The more your score, the better your luck.
